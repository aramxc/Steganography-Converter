What is Steganography?

From Wikipedia:

"Steganography (pronounced STEG-ə-NOG-rə-fee ) is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video. The word steganography combines the Greek words steganos (στεγανός), meaning 'covered, concealed, or protected,' and graphein (γράφειν) meaning 'writing'."

At a basic level, Steganography on the web involves taking an image you wish to conceal, grabbing each pixel's significant digits (i.e. the first 3 digits of a hex color code) and setting those values as the least significant digits (i.e. the last 3 digits of a hex color code) for each pixel of the image you wish to conceal it within.
